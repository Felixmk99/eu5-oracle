Croatia is a small
Catholic
kingdom in the western Balkans that is currently the junior partner in a personal union with
Hungary
. The historic territory of Croatia is divided between Croatia itself and their Hungarian partner.

plausible
formable and as such, if annexed, can be reformed by any country with a
Croatian

location
and at least 50% of locations in the Croatia and Slavonia
areas
