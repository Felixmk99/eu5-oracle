Located southwest of Japan and northeast of Taiwan, Nanzan is a starting country in Europa Universalis 5. It is a single-location country with County rank. It starts in 1337 with Ououso Ōzato as its ruler. Its primary culture is Ryūkyū culture, its religion is Utaki, and its common language, court language, and liturgical language are all Ryūkyū. It is neighbored to the north by the counties of
Chūzan
and
Hokuzan
, and the unclaimed Satsunan Islands, and to the south by the the unclaimed Sakishima Islands, all of which sharing Ryūkyū culture and Utaki.
