Each law has to have a
Policy
selected for it, else it is not active. Different
Government Types
and
Religions
gain different laws, and more laws are given through
Advances
. Depending on what
Policy
we pick in a law, we will often impact our
Societal Values
.
Each country has an Administrative Efficiency, which ranges from 0% to 100%. By default, law changes take 2 years to implement, reduced by the administrative efficiency of the nation enacting the law.
Changing most laws has a base cost of 100
Stability.
Laws may be changed with no stability cost through
Parliament
request, at the cost of 50% approval in Parliament.
