This navbox should contain all the countries with pages on this wiki. The countries are ordered by continent at the tab level, and then by subcontinent within each tab. A country should be listed under the subcontinent where its capital is located. Please add a country to this navbox if you create a new country page. The countries should be entered into the navbox with the form:
{{Navbox group|'Subcontinent name'| {{countries navbox/country|'country 1'}} {{countries navbox/country|'country 2'}} }}
The subtemplates that contain the tabs are located at:
Template:Countries navbox/Africa
Template:Countries navbox/America
Template:Countries navbox/Asia
Template:Countries navbox/Europe
Template:Countries navbox/Oceania
The first tab listed can be changed from default by entering one of the continents as a parameter of the navbox transclusion. For example, on a page about a country in Asia, adding {{Countries navbox|Asia}} will list Asia as the first tab instead of Africa.
