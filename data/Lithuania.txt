Situated between the lands of
Poland
and the mighty
Golden Horde
, the
Grand Duchy of Lithuania
have a proud history as one of the last bastions of Paganism. Under the Gediminaitis dynasty, the Lithuanian people have tasted success by expanding their domain to envelop parts of the Kievan Rus'. However. growing pressure and an ongoing crusade spearheaded by the
Teutonic Order
, the
Livonian Order
and the King of
Bohemia
serve as grave reminders that religious friction may yet contribute to their demise. Various attempts to embrace the
Catholic
faith have been unsuccessful, and raising tensions across our borders threaten out stability. It is in this tense and precarious environment that we must determine our next steps...
