Albania
is a
Catholic
monarchy in the Balkans region. In 1337, it is a vassal of
Naples
and ruled by the Anjou dynasty in 1337. Albania itself has the vassals
Arianiti
,
Mataranga
,
Muzaka
,
Thopia
. The rest of the Albania area is divided between
Serbia
in the north and
Epirus
and
Byzantium
in the south.
